📊 Data Professionals Survey

📌 Overview

This dashboard is culminated from key findings of 630 respondents. Here I've considered the average age, salary by job type, and happiness with work-life balance and salary of data professionals while showcasing the distribution of respondents by country, gender etc providing a snapshot of the data landscape.

Key Features:

  • Visual summary of key data professional demographics.
  • Average age and salary insights by job type.
  • Work-life balance and salary happiness metrics.
  • Respondent distribution by country and gender.
  • Customizable filters for respondent segmentation.

Benefits:

  • Provides a clear snapshot of the data professional landscape.
  • Facilitates understanding of salary benchmarks by role.
  • Highlights work-life balance and satisfaction trends.
  • Enables demographic analysis of data professionals.
  • Supports data-driven decisions related to talent management.
