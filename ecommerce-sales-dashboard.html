🛒 Ecommerce Sales Dashboard

📌 Overview

This Ecommerce Sales Dashboard tracks key metrics like units sold, revenue, refunds, and sponsor costs which provides insights into hourly sales performance, top-selling products, and monthly revenue trends, allowing for quick analysis of sales performance and identification of key drivers.

Key Features:

  • Hourly sales performance tracking for immediate insights.
  • Analysis of top-selling products to identify key drivers.
  • Monthly revenue trend visualization for strategic planning.
  • Monitoring of units sold, revenue, refunds, and sponsor costs.
  • Interactive filters for detailed data exploration.

Benefits:

  • Enables rapid assessment of sales performance and trends.
  • Facilitates quick identification of high-performing products.
  • Supports informed decisions to optimize marketing spend.
  • Provides a comprehensive view of financial performance.
  • Helps in understanding the impact of refunds on overall revenue.
