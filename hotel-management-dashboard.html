This dashboard tracks the performance of a hotel with key metrics like bookings, revenue, and cancellation rates, broken down by month, country, year, and lead time.

Key Features:

  • Interactive filters for country, year, and lead time analysis.
  • Visual representation of total bookings and revenue trends over time.
  • Clear display of average cancellation rate and associated revenue loss.
  • Detailed breakdown of bookings and revenue by distribution channel.
  • Visual insights into average room tariff based on booking lead time.

Benefits:

  • Enables quick identification of booking and revenue patterns.
  • Facilitates informed decisions to minimize revenue loss from cancellations.
  • Provides a comprehensive view of channel performance for targeted strategies.
  • Helps optimize pricing based on booking lead time analysis.
  • Streamlines performance monitoring and reporting for hotel management.
